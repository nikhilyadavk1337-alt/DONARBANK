How AI Routing Works

Our routing algorithm is designed to eliminate the "bystander effect" caused by mass email or SMS blasts. When an emergency is triggered, the system calculates a unique Priority Score for every active donor in the network.

The Algorithm

The score is determined in real-time using the following metrics:

  • Geospatial Proximity: Calculated using the Haversine formula. Closer donors receive exponentially higher scores.
  • Trust & Reliability: Donors who have successfully completed donations in the past gain a higher base trust score.
  • Cooldown Status: The system automatically filters out any donor who has donated within the last 90 days.
  • KYC Verification: Verified donors (Aadhaar/DL checked) are prioritized in the queue.

Once scored, the top 3 matches are pinged via WebSockets for instant, zero-latency notification.

Help Center / FAQ

I requested blood, but no one accepted. What now?

If local donors are unavailable, you can use the Priority Signal Boost feature in your dashboard. For ₹20, your request bypasses local routing and is broadcasted to the entire regional network.

How do I verify my identity?

In your Dashboard profile settings, upload a clear picture of your Aadhaar Card or Driving License. Our admins review the KYC queue daily. Once approved, you will receive a "Verified" badge.

Why am I locked out from donating?

For your health and safety, LifeDrop enforces a strict 90-day cooldown period after a successful donation. You will be automatically unlocked once the cooldown period expires.

Donor Guidelines

Donating blood is a noble act, but it must be done safely. Please ensure you meet the following criteria before marking yourself as "Active" on the network.

Basic Requirements

  • You must be between 18 and 65 years of age.
  • Your weight should not be less than 45 kilograms.
  • Your hemoglobin count should not be less than 12.5 g/dl.
  • You must not have donated blood in the last 90 days (3 months).
  • You should be in good health and not suffering from cold, flu, or other infections.

If you are pregnant, nursing, or have undergone major surgery recently, please refrain from donating.
