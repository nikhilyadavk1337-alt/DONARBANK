The Problem Statement

Hospitals and patients often struggle to find blood donors during medical emergencies, leading to delays in treatment. Our objective is to develop a digital platform that efficiently connects blood donors, hospitals, and patients.

Core Features Built

  • Donor registration and comprehensive blood group database
  • Nearby blood bank locator utilizing high-precision GPS
  • Instant emergency blood request alerts directly to donors
